Findings

How is the path property used in APIs?

  • The average number of path per API is 11.2, ranging from 1 to 310.
  • Out of the 11,678 APIs with a path, 6,416 (54.9%) have 1-5 paths, 1,860 (15.9%) have 6-10 paths, 1,779 (15.2%) have 11-20 paths, 1,147 (9.8%) have 21-50 paths, and 476 (0.0407604%) have over 50 paths.

Methodology

Results are based on the content and count of entries in the /paths property in the APIs specifications.
